You probably do not browse the entire emoji picker every time you react in live chat. Most of us reach for the same handful, so Chat Enhancer now puts them one hover away.

## Your favorites, right where you need them

Hover over YouTube's emoji button and your most-used reactions appear above the message box. Choose one and keep chatting. The list learns from the emojis you use, including YouTube's custom emoji, while the full picker stays one click away.

## Private by default

Your emoji habits stay in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or shared, and the popover fits YouTube's light, dark, and Aero themes.
