**Stick Around!** turns the live chat feed into a tiny arena where two stick figures fight while message bubbles fall from above. The video, chat header, and message box stay in place.

## It's raining bubbles

In Stick Around!, comments are the weather.

Quiet chats produce a few falling bubbles; busy chats create a storm. Bubbles can drift, spin, fall at different speeds, and display text from the local chat.

Move, jump, knock the other player around, and dodge the falling chat without losing all your stocks.

## A different kind of Playground game

Unlike other Playground games, Stick Around! draws the match over the feed instead of using a compact panel. Chat remains visible underneath, and the usual hide and sound controls stay in the header.

## Play against a viewer or Computer

Open the Games panel, choose Stick Around!, and invite someone in the same stream—or invite **Computer** if nobody else is available.

Both players press **Ready**, the countdown starts, and then the match begins.

## How to win

Each player has three stocks. Hits raise your damage, which makes later hits send you farther. Fall out of the arena and you lose a stock; lose all three and the other player wins.

## Why this belongs in chat

Stick Around! makes chat part of the match: the faster the conversation moves, the more dangerous the arena becomes. It is quick, chaotic, and shaped by the stream around it.
