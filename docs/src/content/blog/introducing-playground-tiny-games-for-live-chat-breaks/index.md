## Playground is here!

Playground is a games hub that lives in the extension, where you can play games with other users that also have the extension installed.

Is your streamer on a bathroom break? Open Playground until they’re back.

:::media-right

![Compact chess game panel floating over YouTube live chat](./f779ebcc57f8280d.png){shadow=smooth rotation=-2}

Games are compact and won’t get much bigger than this. The panel is draggable, so you can place them anywhere in chat.

The first game available is good old chess. How fast can you checkmate your new stream buddy?

:::

Look for the Playground options in this section of the extension settings.

:::media-left

![Playground settings in the Chat Enhancer popup](./c99cee6a87fb4253.png)

Enable the “Join Playground” setting for the Games icon to appear in chat.

When you open the Games panel, you’ll have to enable the “Available for games” option, so other players can see you, or enable the “Available by default” option in the extension settings so that it’s always on. Up to you.

:::
